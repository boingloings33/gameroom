About the store

Game Room is a Charleston local gaming shop that offers a large variety of modern and retro gaming supplies at a very affordable price. We have been running strong for two years, offering games and consoles along with any accessory you could ever dream of. We also have various arcade and pinball machines along with gaming trivia.

Why choose us?

Game Room is locally owned and directly supports the Charleston gaming community. We run weekly tournaments and charity events to bring like minded gamers together. We also offer a loyalty program and membership perks. Come by and check us out, we're excited to see you!
